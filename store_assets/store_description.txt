TAB GROUPS & WINDOWS LIST

Simplify your tab management with a clear, hierarchical overview of your browser.

Are you a power user who relies on Chrome's Tab Groups to stay organized? Tab Groups & Windows List is a minimalist productivity tool designed to give you an instant, bird's-eye view of all your active groups across every open window.

KEY FEATURES

Instant 2-Level Overview: See all your tab groups in a clean, expandable list.

Window Mapping: Click any group to reveal exactly which browser window it is currently in.

Visual Consistency: Automatically matches your group colors (Blue, Red, Yellow, Green, etc.) for intuitive recognition.

Lightweight and Fast: No background processes, no tracking, and zero impact on browser performance.

Privacy Focused: Requires only the minimum necessary permissions to function.

WHY USE THIS EXTENSION?

When you have dozens of tabs spread across multiple windows, finding a specific group can be a challenge. This extension eliminates the "window hunting" by providing a centralized directory. Whether you are managing work projects, research, or social tabs, you can now locate your groups in seconds.

HOW IT WORKS

Click the extension icon in your toolbar.

View the list of all active tab groups.

Expand a group to see its host window name or ID.

Navigate with confidence knowing exactly where your tabs are.

PERMISSIONS NOTE

This extension requires access to tabGroups, tabs, and windows APIs solely to generate the list and help you navigate. Your data never leaves your browser.
